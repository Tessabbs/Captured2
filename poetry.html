  • Manage me to tears
    I’m just trying,
    Not cheating or lying,
    You mean well with your spying,
    And yet still I’m crying.
    I’m following my heart,
    And why shouldn’t I start?
    I promise I’m grateful,
    But I’ve had more than my plateful,
    I’m managing my best to keep on going,
    I’m trying my hardest to keep on growing,
    I’m sorry that you don’t like that my tactics are showing.

  • A glimmer
    Slowly breaking through, peering through the cracks,
    tentative steps forward, no more panic attacks,
    Attentively listening, but this time I hear,
    focus, understanding, all of me here,
    Billowing laughter fills my lungs,
    excited chatter frees my tongue,
    Legs walking, straight, tall,
    it’s the moment that matters and that is all.

  • Limits
    I’ve tried, I’ve stressed
    I’ve wailed, I’ve rest,
    I’ve reached my limit, this is it,
    I can’t go on, I feel like shit,
    This feels like the end, the end of forever,
    Stripped of my wits and no longer clever,
    Can’t look back, unable to move,
    Pathetically desperate, this has to improve.

  • Me
    And I know it’s hard to see
    what it is like to be me.
    Everything seems fine
    but my life’s on the line.
    You see my pretty painted facade,
    but keeping it up is ever so hard.
    It’s starting to crumble, starting to crack,
    weak and waiting for you to attack.

  • Stormy waves crash and break,
    continual pounding my head must take:
    Meaningless nonsense, daggers scythe,
    restless rumpus, thoughts that writhe.
    Too much, too fast, can’t rationalise,
    confusion, contusions, falling skies.
    Outside voice, a vicious attack,
    helpful suggestions widen the crack.
    Reach for reality, confound the panic,
    behaviour irrational, increasingly manic.
    Helpful words – more info to assess,
    twisted and tangled, can’t process,
    Want to be nice, be friendly, polite,
    don’t want to push you away, reject you with spite.

  • You know I think I love you, And I think you love me to,
    So why is it I still feel that I don’t know what to do?
    I’m so scared and so afraid, afraid of what might be,
    So scared of all the people, with whom I’ll never get to see,
    I’m so frightened of the static, of taking just one way,
    The dynamism of my fluid self and sexuality will surely come to play.

  • And I can’t change,
    Even if I tried, even if I wanted to,
    No I can’t change.
